About Me

Hi, nice to meet you! My name is Margot and I am the blogger behind this website. 19 years old, Antwerp based, studying for graphic designer at AP.
And absolutely obsessed by everything fashion, beauty or food related. I love traveling, chai tea lattes, fresh raspberry juice, (obsessed with) London,
taking pictures, of course shopping and window shopping. I started this site to collect, share and write about the things I love.
I hope you have fun reading my articles and looking at my work.
